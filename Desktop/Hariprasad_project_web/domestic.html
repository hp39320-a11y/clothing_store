Mysore, located in Karnataka, is a city rich in history and culture, known for its royal heritage and stunning architecture. The iconic Mysore Palace, with its grand design and intricate artwork, stands as a testament to the city's royal past. Mysore is also famous for its annual Dussehra festival, a grand celebration of art, culture, and tradition. Other attractions include the Chamundeshwari Temple atop Chamundi Hill, the serene Brindavan Gardens, and the Mysore Zoo.
